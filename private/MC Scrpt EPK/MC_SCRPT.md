# MC SCRPT EPK

## ARTIST BACKGROUND
Philip Saa is an American MC based in Germany, representing jungle and drum & bass with a distinct, authentic voice. Inspired by New Forms by Roni Size, he developed a style rooted in 90s West Coast hip-hop, sharp wordplay, and dynamic live delivery.
Having earned his place within established crews like Monsters of Jungle and Booyaka, he brings both respect for the scene and proven mic presence. Known for his rich tone and natural crowd control, MC SCRPT delivers high-energy performances, reading the room and elevating the DJ’s journey from start to finish.
